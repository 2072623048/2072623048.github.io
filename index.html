About Us

At Grand Loong, we are passionate about revolutionizing education through cutting-edge hardware and technology. As a leading educational hardware development company, we specialize in creating innovative tools that enhance learning and teaching experiences. Our mission is to empower educators and students with advanced, hands-on solutions that bridge the gap between theory and practice.

Join Our Team

Grand Loong is expanding its horizons and is currently recruiting for the following positions:

Automation Engineer

  • Bachelor's degree or higher in automation control or related field, with at least 2 years of relevant experience.
  • Proficient in at least one PLC system (SIEMENS, SCHNEIDER, AB, OMRON) and capable of independent design and programming.
  • Proficient in at least one SCADA software (WINCC, IFIX, INTOUCH, CITECT, Kingview) and capable of independent design and programming.
  • Familiar with PLC network structures and fieldbus systems.
  • Strong communication skills, proactive attitude, and team spirit.

Hardware Engineer

  • Bachelor's degree or higher in communication, automation, electronics, or related field, with at least 2 years of relevant experience.
  • Expertise in analog/digital circuit design and microcontroller design, with knowledge of low-level software programming.
  • Strong learning ability, hardworking, and team-oriented.

VR Developer

  • Bachelor's degree or higher in computer science, software engineering, or related field, with experience in VR development.
  • Proficient in VR platforms such as Unity, Unreal Engine, or similar.
  • Experience in 3D modeling, animation, and interactive design for VR applications.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work in a collaborative team environment.
  • Passion for educational technology and creating immersive learning experiences.

International Marketing Engineer

  • At least 1 year of experience in foreign trade, familiar with Alibaba platform.
  • Strong foreign language writing and speaking skills, able to communicate fluently with international clients.
  • Excellent communication, execution, and negotiation skills, with a strong sense of responsibility and teamwork.

Marketing Specialist

  • Bachelor's degree or higher in marketing, business administration, or related field, with at least 1 year of marketing experience.
